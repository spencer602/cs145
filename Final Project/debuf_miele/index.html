Carbiodrati Italiano opened its doors in 1993 in the basement of a Minneapolis apartment building. Our mission is to capture the fun and enthusiasm of an authentic Italian gathering along with delectable food. Carbiodrati Italiano restaurant offers an eclectic setting designed to make every occasion a party! Our dishes are inspierd by the cuisine enjoyed for generations in villages throughout Italy. Our dishes are served family-style, meant to be sharted by the entire table. Nothing brings a group closer together than trying a bit of this and a taste of that.
